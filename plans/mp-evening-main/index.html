Evening in Mt. Pleasant

2-4 HOURS · 8 STOPS · MOUNT PLEASANT, VANCOUVER

Tap a stop to read more — open the guide to customize this route.

If you've been looking for a neighbourhood to grab dinner, hang out in a park, or bar after dinner, Mt. Pleasant could be a great option. In this guide I'll give you a bunch of options for dinner, drinks, and hang out options. Mount Pleasant has so many great restaurants, bars, and venues, so it's really hard to come up with a cohesive list, but as of right now, these are some of my favourite spots. If I was heading in for dinner, I'd first stop off at 33a for a beer or non-alcoholic beverage, they do both, extraordinarily well, and their patio is large with a pretty quick turn-over. If they are too busy, you can always try Brassneck if beer is what you are after.

Then, I would head to either AJs for pizza, Como for tapas, or Sushi Hil for sushi. Depending on your budget and cuisine preference, you cannot go wrong with any of those choices.

After dinner, there are lots of options if you are looking to stick around the area for a bit longer and grab another couple of drinks or bites. If you are wanting to stay close to AJs or Como, you can head to Mount Pleasant Vintage, who's always got great cocktails and a good energy. If you are at Sushi Hil, then within a stones throw is Good Thief for cocktails, or if you want a real unique experience, head up to Shameful Tiki Room for a one of a kind night.
