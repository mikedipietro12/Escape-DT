Quick Sip & Shop (Stay Close to Train)

45-60 MINS · 5 STOPS · COMMERCIAL DRIVE, VANCOUVER

Tap a stop to read more — open the guide to customize this route.

Once you get off the Skytrain, head north towards the mountains. Stop in at JJ bean and grab a chocolate chip cookie (or a Krispie raisin), grab a quick shot of espresso.

1. JJ Bean

If you wanted to spend a bit more time (or need a second coffee), head up to Prado for another drink and some people watching on the corner.

2. Prado Cafe

You can pop into Audiopile or Canterbury Tales if you want to take a look, but if time is really limited, keep walking down to Mintage.

3. Audiopile

4. Canterbury Tales

In Mintage, you will find some amazing vintage clothing, from t-shirts, to vintage dresses, cowboy boots to running vests. An amazing spot to just walk around even if you're not shopping.

5. Mintage
