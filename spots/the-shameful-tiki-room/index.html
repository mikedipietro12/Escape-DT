The Shameful Tiki Room

Main St & 28th Ave
cocktailsbarsit down$$drinks47 min from Main Street–Science World

Looking for a unique cocktail experience? Shameful Tiki room is for you. Dark, mysterious, and a ton of fun. Service is hilarious and the cocktails are too. A must try if you are looking for a fun late night out.

My go-to: Volcano bowl (with friends).
