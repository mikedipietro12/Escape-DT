Pantitlán

E Hastings St & Nanaimo · $$ · drinks, food

The newest, most popular taco spot in the city. If you don't mind waiting, this former hidden gem, tucked away behind a Mexican bakery, serves some authentic Mexican cocktails and tacos.

My go-to: pastor and suedero with a marg and beer chaser.
