LIVIA Forno e Vino

Kitchener
cocktailsbaked goodscookiesitaliansit downgrab and gopatiolunchdinner$$coffeefood15 min from Commercial–Broadway

One of the cutest coffee shops in the neighborhood. Great patio with an amazing piazza next door where you can sit with your take out if the restaurant is too busy (it usually is!). Amazing baked goods made in house every day, a great place for a coffee, a treat, lunch, or to take home some bread.

My go-to: Breakfast sandwich with mortadella and an americano
