Havana

Charles
sit downpatiocubancocktailslunchdinner$$drinksfood20 min from Commercial–Broadway

One of the best patios in the neighborhood, lots of space and great people watching. Food is good, drinks are cold, and vibes are high.

My go-to: Pitcher of sangria or margs on the patio. Cubano or fish tacos if you're hungry.
