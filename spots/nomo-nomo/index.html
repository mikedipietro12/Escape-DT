Nomo Nomo

Charles & Commercial · $$$ · food, drinks

A newer Izakaya on the block, this location has some of the best cocktails in the city. Some serious chemistry going on in here to get some wild cocktails. Their super sharable plates are awesome for dining with 2-6 people, make a reso, this place fills up fast!

My go-to: Wagyu sliders and a tonka cocktail
