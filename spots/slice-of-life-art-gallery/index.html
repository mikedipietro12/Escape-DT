Slice of Life Art Gallery

Venables · $–$$$ · shopping, hangout

Part art-gallery, part shop, part co-working space, photo booth, part vintage shop this place has it all. It is a must stop if you make it to Venables from the skytrain, there is truly something for everyone. Local gifts made by local artists of all shapes and sizes.

My go-to: Look through the stickers, art books, head to the vintage shop, then photo booth before taking in what's in the gallery that week.
