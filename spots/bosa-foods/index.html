Bosa Foods

Victoria & Turner st
grab and gopatiocoffeeitaliansandwicheshot table$$foodgroceries35 min from Commercial–Broadway

Bosa is a local grocer specializing in Italian goods. Stop in for a sandwich or something from the hot table. Also a good option to grab some euro groceries if you are planning on cooking at home.

My go-to: Lupini, biscotti, and a meatball sandwich
