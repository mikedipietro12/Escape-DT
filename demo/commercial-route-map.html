Current (live app)

Black spine runs from Venables (y=50) to the station. ViewBox stays at the full 340×600 canvas when the station is shown.

Fixed (prototype)

Spine and viewBox clip to the route — station plus stops only, with a little padding. Stops fill the map frame.

What we're testing

  • Spine bounds — getMapSpineBounds should use route stop Y positions (+ station) instead of always yNorth…yStation when the station is on the map.
  • ViewBox — computeMapViewBox should run for station-start routes too, not only when !showStation.
  • South-of-station stops — Equinox, Flourist, and The Burrow sit below the station on the map (y > 550). The live spine used to end at the station, so E 18th hung off the line. Cropping the spine to route bounds fixes that too.
  • Round-trip legs — coloured paths and backward (gold) segments should still draw correctly inside the cropped frame.
