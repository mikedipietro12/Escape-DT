What we're testing

  • Lane bump — each westward leg steps the route line up one lane (18px).
  • Turnaround curves — backtrack bulges east and rises; resuming east bulges west, curves down, and rejoins the Hastings spine.
  • Cross-street columns — spine stops with the same label (e.g. two Kamloops) share one vertical map column.
  • Dots above line — stop markers sit on top of the route stroke, not centered on it.
