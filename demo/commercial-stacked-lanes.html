Commercial Drive map — stacked lanes (archived experiment) Superseded by hybrid paths for the live app. Kept for comparison: every reversal steps further left; lanes never rejoin the spine.

Map style: Hybrid (off-spine curves only) · Curved lanes (rejoins spine on north) · Stacked lanes

What we're testing

  • Every reversal — any north↔south change bumps one lane left (18px); same-direction legs stay on the current lane.
  • North→south — upward bulge, curve onto the next lane left, then continue south.
  • South→north — downward bulge, curve onto the next lane left (not back to the spine).
  • Ribbon stack — a zigzag route fans out west like folded paper; try the backtrack preset for three lanes.
  • Off-spine — Victoria and Venables legs still use rounded corners from the active lane.
  • Trace arrow — open chevron leads the drawing tip.
