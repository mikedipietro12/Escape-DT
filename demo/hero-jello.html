Hero animation lab — jello poke-through

Drag your mouse through the mascot: pixels get pushed aside around the cursor like jello, then spring back into shape. Demo only — see also the intro variants, blob & deform, and combined demos.

How it works: the mascot PNG is drawn on a small WebGL canvas (raw WebGL, no three.js). A fragment shader displaces pixels radially around the cursor; the push vector lives in a spring-damper simulation, so when the cursor stops or leaves, the dent oscillates back to rest — that’s the jello. Wobbliness lowers the damping (longer jiggle); Push strength scales how hard cursor velocity shoves the pixels. Touch works too — drag a finger across the mascot.
