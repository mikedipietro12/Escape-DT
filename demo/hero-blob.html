Hero animation lab — blob & deform

The mascot squashes, stretches and warps with scroll velocity, and wobbles when you mouse over (or poke) it. Scroll the page to feel it. Demo only — see also the intro variants and the jello poke-through demos.

Deform style

Tuning

Jelly maps scroll velocity to squash & stretch transforms and snaps back with an elastic.out tween when scrolling stops; hover triggers the same wobble. Goo warp pushes scroll velocity into an SVG feTurbulence + feDisplacementMap filter so the PNG genuinely melts and re-forms. (Note: SVG displacement filters on CSS filter are flaky in Safari — jelly is the safe cross-browser pick; goo would need a fallback.)

Keep scrolling

Fast flicks stretch the mascot more than slow drags — the deformation is velocity-based, not position-based. Stop abruptly and it springs back into shape.

Why velocity?

Position-based effects feel like a scrubbed video; velocity-based ones feel like physics. The mascot reads as soft and alive without ever leaving its spot in the layout.

Try the modes

Switch to Goo warp and flick-scroll: the edges of the PNG ripple like jelly. "Both" layers the transform squash on top of the filter warp.

Hover, too

Mouse over the mascot (or hit Poke) for a quick squash-and-wobble. On touch devices a tap does the same.
