What we're testing

  • Horizontal spine — E Hastings runs left (west) to right (east), opposite of Commercial Drive's north–south map.
  • Off-spine legs — stops like Parallel 49 and Pandora Park use L-shaped paths (mirroring Victoria off Commercial).
  • No station — East Village omits SkyTrain; walking totals are linear between stops only.
  • Stacked layout — map full width on top; route cards appear centered underneath after the animation.
  • Angled labels — visit number + side street (e.g. 1. Semlin); same pin merges (1. & 3. Semlin); off-spine stops use the horizontal street (3. Triumph St at Triumph & Victoria, not Victoria).
  • Gold segments — legs that backtrack west use the same backward colour as Commercial Drive.
