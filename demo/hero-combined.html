Layering: the mascot lives on a WebGL canvas (jello shader, spring physics); GSAP animates the canvas element for the intro pop and idle breathing, while the shader warps the pixels inside it — the two never fight. The speech bubble and its text stay in the DOM, animated with the same elastic timeline as the intro-variants demo.
