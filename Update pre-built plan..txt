(Or title: Quick Sip & Shop)

Title: Quick Sip & Shop (Stay Close to Train)
Duration: 45-60 mins
Description: If you have under an hour, here's a quick taste of what Commercial has to offer.

Introduction:
Once you get off the Skytrain, head north towards the mountains. Stop in at JJ bean and grab a chocolate chip cookie (or a Krispie raisin), grab a quick shot of espresso. If you wanted to spend a bit more time (or need a second coffee), head up to Prado for another drink and some people watching on the corner. You can pop into Audiopile or Canterbury Tales if you want to take a look, but if time is really limited, keep walking down to Mintage. In Mintage, you will find some amazing vintage clothing, from t-shirts, to vintage dresses, cowboy boots to running vests. An amazing spot to just walk around even if you're not shopping.

Stops in order :
1. JJ Bean — coffee + cookie first
2. Prado Cafe — sit and people-watch
3. Audiopile - Record shopping
4. Canterbury Tales - Used books
3. Mintage — quick vintage browse

Title: Quick Sip & Shop 2 (Start in the North)
Duration: 45-60 mins
Description: Starting at the opposite end of the Skytrain and working your way back to the train. Coffee, shopping, art, and a nice walk.

Introduction:
If you have the ability to start in the north end, this would probably be my preferred quick stop. Start by popping into Alterior, in there you can check out locally made clothing and some specially curated brands you've never seen anywhere else. While you are in Alterior, you can also check out Vintage Sponsor; easily one of the best vintage shops in the city, I can't even go in there without buying something. Head upstairs and check out Book Section, a free library and book shop. From there, head over to Bump N Grind for a quick clover coffee, and if you have the time, pop into Slice of Life or Fun Haus. Slice if you are looking to browse some art and some locally made gifts. Fun Haus if you want to see some collectabiles and more vintange clothing. At this point, it's likely time to head towards the train, which would be about a 20 minute walk south where you will be tempted by so many more spots!

Stops in order:
1. Alterior
2. Vintage Sponsor
3. Book Section
4. Bump N Grind
5. Slice of Life
6. Fun Haus
